# TelAviv2030 Group Savings Agreement

**Effective Date:** [Date of signing]

## Preamble

This agreement is entered into between the members of TelAviv2030 (the "Group") for the purpose of establishing a communal savings system to fund group activities, trips, and shared expenses.

## 1. Communal Ownership

By contributing money to the TelAviv2030 group savings, I understand and agree that:

- All money deposited becomes **communal property** of the Group
- I will **not have individual ownership** of shares or specific amounts
- Funds are managed collectively for the benefit of all Group members
- No member has a right to withdraw their "share" individually

## 2. Cashout Process

All withdrawals from the group savings (cashouts) require:

- A formal proposal submitted through the TelAviv2030 platform
- Group approval through voting process
- Unanimous consent from all active members, with a 48-hour timeout for absent members
- Documentation of the intended use (receipts, product links, etc.)

## 3. Decision Making

- All members have equal voting weight regardless of contribution amount
- The 48-hour voting window allows for reasonable time to consider proposals
- Absent members after 48 hours are considered to have abstained
- Disputes will be resolved through group discussion

## 4. Liability Disclaimer

I understand and agree that:

- This platform is **not a financial institution**
- The platform maintainers (administrators) are **not liable** for disputes between members
- This is a voluntary arrangement between friends
- No guarantees are made about returns or security beyond best efforts
- Participation is entirely voluntary and can be discontinued by withdrawing from the Group

## 5. Privacy

- Member information (name, email, contribution history) is visible to other Group members
- Financial data is shared transparently within the Group
- No data will be shared with third parties except as required by law

## 6. Amendments

- This agreement may be amended by unanimous consent of all Group members
- Proposed amendments must be submitted and voted on like cashout proposals

## 7. Termination

- A member may withdraw from the Group at any time
- Upon withdrawal, the member forfeits any claim to the communal savings
- The remaining members may vote on whether to return a portion of the withdrawn member's contributions, subject to the Group's financial status

## 8. Acceptance

By signing this agreement electronically, I confirm that:

- I have read and understood all terms
- I agree to be bound by this agreement
- I am participating voluntarily
- I am of legal age to enter into this agreement
